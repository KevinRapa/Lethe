When prompted for input, the format may appear in angle brackets.            <item> - Enter an item name.                    <left> - Left arrow key.   <'x'> - Enter what's quoted.                   <#> - Enter a digit.       < > - Enter nothing.       <(?)> - An optional item or item slot.        <?/?/?> - A choice of ?.   <'s' list> - 's' followed by a comma separated list.                          Hot keys which you may find useful: Press 'alt' or 'option' (on a Mac) to swap the inventory and dialog panels. The left and right arrow keys can be used to scroll up and down if scroll bars appear. You may also use the up arrow key to browse previous input and the down arrow key to clear the input panel.
